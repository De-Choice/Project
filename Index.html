My First Webpage

The moment man sinned, he lost his identity in God and became alien to God. All that he used to enjoy in God
including the presence of God became something he didn't want to have again because he was now a stranger to
God (Gen 3:10). The being that God says should have dominion now becomes fearful because of sin. and we see
God making attempts to restore that initial relationship with man all to no avail. Part of the attempts of God was
animal blood used as sacrifices for sin but that could still not make a man free from sin. Until this prophecy by
Isaiah became fulfilled in Jesus. Jesus came and broke every limitation between us and God by his death on the
cross. By His death, we became acceptable to God. All that Jesus suffered was not because He was a sinner. All
the mockery, pain, humiliation He suffered was as a result of him taking upon Himself our iniquities. Christ was
obedient to the father even till death. How obedient are we to Him who took our place and died so that we can live.
As we honour and remember all these that Jesus went through, let's examine our lives and answer this question within
us. Is Jesus' death not or becoming a waste over my life?
